# Of Boys and Men - Richard Reeves

**Category**: Books

**Where to buy**: [Galaxus](https://www.galaxus.ch/search?q=Of+Boys+and+Men+Richard+Reeves) | [Ex Libris](https://www.exlibris.ch/)

> An examination of the challenges facing boys and men in modern society, exploring educational, economic, and social struggles. Reeves presents data-driven analysis of how boys are falling behind in schools and the workforce, whilst offering practical policy proposals to address male disadvantage without undermining progress towards gender equality. A balanced and nuanced look at masculinity in the 21st century.
