> The Pulitzer Prize-winning true story of General Alex Dumas, father of novelist Alexandre Dumas. Born in slavery in the French colony of Saint-Domingue, he rose to command armies during the French Revolution, becoming the highest-ranking man of African descent ever to lead a European army. A remarkable tale of adventure, discrimination, and forgotten heroism.
