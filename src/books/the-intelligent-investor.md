> Widely regarded as the definitive book on value investing, this classic text by Benjamin Graham has educated and inspired investors since 1949. Graham introduces the concept of "Mr. Market" and emphasises the importance of a margin of safety, disciplined analysis, and long-term thinking over speculation. Warren Buffett has called it "by far the best book on investing ever written," making it essential reading for anyone seeking to build wealth through intelligent, patient investment strategies.
