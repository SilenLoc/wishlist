> A collection of 366 daily meditations on fatherhood, drawing from history, philosophy, and literature to offer practical wisdom on raising children. Ryan Holiday explores timeless lessons about patience, discipline, leading by example, and being present for one's children. Each day provides thoughtful reflections to help fathers become better parents and role models, inspired by Stoic philosophy and the experiences of great fathers throughout history.
