> Psychiatrist Viktor Frankl's memoir of his experiences as an Auschwitz concentration camp inmate and his psychotherapeutic method of finding meaning in all forms of existence, even the most brutal. Frankl argues that we cannot avoid suffering but we can choose how to cope with it, find meaning in it, and move forward with renewed purpose.
